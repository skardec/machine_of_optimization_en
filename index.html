    About Me

    I am an applied mathematician, graduated and post-graduated in optimization (Master’s degree), with extensive knowledge in linear algebra, calculus, differential geometry, linear programming (as well as operations research), and in this way I work in several adjacent fields such as computer science, statistics, physics, engineering, etc. My role is to solve problems that can be mathematically modeled. In other words, I am easily adaptable to any subject, always solving problems that one would not even imagine involving mathematics, such as more recent challenges in marketing. In recent years, I have deepened my study of machine learning, as a Python developer, where I now know how to model problems and implement every step of neural networks for classification and regression problems (whether linear, logistic, non-linear, etc.) and now I am seeking new challenges in applied mathematics, so that I can use my knowledge for mutual benefit.
